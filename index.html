01 // Profile

I'm a versatile professional based in Waterloo, Ontario, with a career that has moved across electronics engineering, IT network and systems administration, precision manufacturing, healthcare support, and financial services.

I currently work as a self-employed Financial Advisor and Marketing Director with World Financial Group (WFG), holding an LLQP licence to advise on life insurance, savings, and investment products for individuals and families. Alongside this, I've worked as a Production Associate at Teledyne DALSA, building precision camera and image sensors in a cleanroom environment for medical, military, aerospace, and space applications — including a camera assembly used in a NASA rocket program.

My earlier career includes supervising network services and electronic systems as a System Supervisor in India, and supporting clients directly as a Healthcare Support Worker in Ontario. I hold a Postgraduate Certificate in Applied Network Infrastructure and System Administration from Conestoga College, and a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Mahatma Gandhi University, Kerala.

Across every role, the common thread is the same: understanding how a system works, finding where it's exposed, and building something that holds up — whether that system is a network, a sensor, a financial plan, or a care routine.
